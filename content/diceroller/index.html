Board Game Dice Roller

Have you ever been frustrated how unfair dice could be? How many games have you lost because of this?

This app will bring equity and fairness in your games!

With Board Game Dice Roller all your dice rolls will be judged by fair random.

Currently supported games:

  • Backgammon
  • Monopoly
  • Munchkin
  • Formula D
  • Settlers of Catan
  • Settlers of Catan: Cities & Knights
  • Stone Age
  • Merchants & Marauders
  • Dice Poker (Yatzy)
  • Evolution
  • Evolution: Continents
