## <img src="/images/rothenburg-ob-der-tauber.svg" class="inline-logo" /> Rothenburg ob der Tauber

In March 2017 we've visited medieval german city of [Rothenburg ob der Tauber](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rothenburg_ob_der_Tauber). At [Untere Schmiedgasse 2](https://goo.gl/maps/maQ4EAtYgsK2) we've found a small art gallery selling hundred year old postcards. I couldn't resist buying some. Here they are.

<figure><img src="/images/2017/rodt-007.jpg" width="1000" /><figcaption><p>Rothenburg ob der Tauber, Herrengasse</p></figcaption></figure>

The city is still very much the same as on these postcards and attracts thousand of tourists daily.

Few postcards featured original drawings instead of photos.

<figure><img src="/images/2017/rodt-004.jpg" width="600" /><figcaption><p>Rothenburg ob der Tauber</p></figcaption></figure>

All postcards come from the private collection of the art gallery owner. Most of them were made by her father.

<figure><img src="/images/2017/rodt-002.jpg" width="1000" /><figcaption><p>Published and photographed by Ernst Geissendörfer, Rothenburg ob der Tauber</p></figcaption></figure>

I regret not buying more of these postcards.
